01 OUR SERVICES

L21UA is a Ukrainian startup specializing in the use of data and artificial intelligence to optimize business processes and increase the efficiency of your company. Our dynamic team comprises skilled experts in data science, machine learning, business analysis, finance, marketing, healthcare, biology and software development, which enables us to deliver customized solutions that meet each client's unique needs.

Furthermore, our company is deeply committed to expanding the boundaries of research and development. One example is the MedTech industry. Through collaboration with top healthcare professionals in Ukraine, we focus on applying machine learning to fields such as dentistry, orthodontics, psychiatry, and psychology. By regularly publishing scientific articles on these topics, we strive to promote cutting-edge knowledge and drive progress in these critical areas.

Our innovative solutions can be applied in any industry, and we are ready to help you develop and implement innovative technologies and methodologies.
